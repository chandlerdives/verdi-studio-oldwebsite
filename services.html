services

We currently offer three packages, all of which work in a similar way. After our initial consultation we'll send you a client questionnaire to find out more about your company and your goals, and book a date for your project to begin. Using the information from your client questionnaire we will come up with a typography selection, full colour palette and a first design for your site.

Using your copy, content and photographs we will build the pages of your website and set up social media and plugins, ensuring you have a consistent and professional design throughout. If you don't have copy, content and photographs we can use placeholders in the meantime.

When your website is ready to go live we can jump on a consultation call and a website walk through.

Take a look below and see which one suits your needs or get in touch to chat about your options.

Landing Page

2-3 weeks

A simple but effective landing page website in an aesthetically pleasing design. It will showcase important information about your business all on one page, making it easy to navigate for your customers.

Included:

  • Consultation + initial design
  • Custom coded website
  • Development of up to one page
  • Initial content importation
  • Mobile responsive and SEO friendly

Medium Site

4-6 weeks

This is a standard size website, with custom-coded pages and plenty of content. We'll maintain a consistent style and design throughout the site, to ensure your branding is at the forefront.

Inclusions:

  • Consultation + initial design
  • Custom coded website
  • Development of up to four pages
  • Initial content importation
  • Mobile responsive and SEO friendly

Large Site

5-7 weeks

If you need a more in-depth site to provide multiple services for your customers, this package is the one for you. With up to eight custom-coded pages, there is plenty of content for your customers to explore.

Inclusions:

  • Consultation + initial design
  • Custom coded website
  • Development of up to eight pages
  • Initial content importation
  • Mobile responsive and SEO friendly
